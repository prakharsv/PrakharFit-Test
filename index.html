From 120 kg to Certified Coach

Three chapters. One life-changing decision at a time.

Chapter 1 — The Weight of It All (Before 2018)

At my heaviest, I weighed 120 kg. My college years were defined by a sedentary routine, minimal sport, and habitual binge eating. The damage wasn't just physical — my confidence was at rock bottom, my grades suffered, and every time I had to represent myself or an institution I belonged to, I held back. Not from a lack of ability, but from the weight of how I saw myself.

Chapter 2 — The 6-Month Shift (2018)

One day, I stopped asking "why me?" and started asking "what now?" I walked into a gym, put myself under a trainer's guidance, and committed 100% — the first time I had ever done that for anything. Within six months, I had lost 25–30 kg. My nutrition shifted from a diet of trans fats to a structured macronutrient-balanced approach. The scale changed. More importantly, so did everything else — my confidence, my clarity, and my sense of what I was capable of.

Chapter 3 — From Advocate to Coach

Years later, I was practicing as an advocate. But the demands of legal work pulled me away from my health, and the stress compounded fast. I asked myself a hard question: why am I investing time in something that returns nothing of what I value most? I looked around and saw people everywhere who wanted to transform their health but had no direction, no system, and no one who truly understood both the science and the human side of change. I enrolled in a certification programme and in six months — echoing that same timeline that first changed my life — I earned my ACE Certified Personal Trainer and Sports Nutrition Specialist certifications. This is my purpose.
