Meera T. - School Teacher, Indore

"My biggest fear was that I would have to give up Indian food or cook separate meals for myself. That never happened. Six months in, I am two dress sizes smaller and, more importantly, I am not exhausted by 2pm anymore. My students noticed before I did."
