    Beyond the number

    BMI Is One Signal. Not the Full Picture.

    A person with high muscle mass may show as "overweight" on BMI while being in excellent health. A person in the "normal" range may carry excess visceral fat. BMI gives you a starting reference. What you do with that information is what matters. A properly designed training and nutrition program, built around your actual body composition, is the next step.
