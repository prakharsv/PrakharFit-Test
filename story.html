My Story

I am not someone who was always fit. I know exactly what it feels like to start from zero, to fail before, and to not know where the floor is.

Chapter 1: 120 kg and No Way Out

At my heaviest, I weighed 120 kg. College had been four years of a sedentary lifestyle, habitual binge eating, and zero accountability. The weight showed up everywhere: in how I carried myself, how I spoke in a room, how I made decisions. I was not just physically heavy. I was stuck. My confidence was at rock bottom and I had no idea where the floor was.

Chapter 2: Six Months That Changed Everything

I stopped waiting to feel ready and walked into a gym. I put myself under a trainer, committed fully for the first time in my life, and learned how nutrition and training actually work together. In six months, I lost 25 to 30 kg. Not through a crash diet or an extreme program, but through a structured, science-backed approach I could sustain.

The scale changed. But so did everything else: my focus, my confidence, and my sense of what I was actually capable of. That transformation was not just about the weight. It was about understanding that the body responds predictably when you give it the right inputs.

Chapter 3: From Advocate to Coach

I spent years as a practicing advocate. The demands of legal work slowly pulled me back away from my health, a pattern I recognised in almost everyone around me. Smart, driven people with no time, no clear direction, and no one who understood both the science and the pressure of a real schedule.

I enrolled in a certification programme and earned my ACE Certified Personal Trainer and Sports Nutrition Specialist credentials in six months, echoing the same timeline that first changed my life. The system I built for myself is now the system I build for others. What changes is how the principles are applied to you.
