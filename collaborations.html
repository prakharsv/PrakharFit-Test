How we can work together

The case for collaborating

Why a Credentialed Coach Outperforms Generic Influencer Reach.

  • Qualified to speak with authorityMy recommendations come from 12 professional certifications and active coaching experience. When I endorse a product, my audience understands I have the training to evaluate it accurately.
  • Trust built over time, not boughtMy audience is engaged because I have been honest and evidence-based. That earned trust makes recommendations more influential than volume-based placements with larger but less engaged followings.
  • Hyper-relevant Indian audienceMy content is built around Indian lifestyles, Indian food, and Indian working conditions. For brands targeting the Indian health and wellness market, this specificity is more valuable than a large but generic reach.
  • Selective by designI limit collaborations to brands I would genuinely use with clients. That selectivity is a signal: a partnership with me carries more weight than one with someone who takes every deal offered.
  • Direct client relationshipsBeyond social platforms, I have direct coaching relationships through WhatsApp and email. A product mention in an active coaching context carries significantly more conversion weight than a social post.
  • Content that holds up to scrutinyI do not write vague promotional copy. Content I create is specific, accurate, and grounded in exercise science and nutrition research. This protects your brand's credibility as much as it protects mine.

What I will not do: I do not endorse products I have not used or would not recommend to a client. I do not promote fat burners, unregulated supplements, detox products, or anything that contradicts evidence-based practice. If your product falls into one of these categories, this is not the right collaboration. If your product is genuinely good, I want to hear from you.
