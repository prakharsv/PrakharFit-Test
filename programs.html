Common Questions

Clarifying the coaching process for you.

I have never lifted weights before. Is this for me?

This is built for you. I started from the same place: 120 kg, no gym experience, no idea where to begin. Every program is custom-built for your current fitness level, so you learn correct form, build confidence, and progress safely without ever feeling overwhelmed.

Will I have to give up Indian food or eat bland meals?

Absolutely not. My own nutrition shifted from binge eating to a macronutrient-balanced diet, and it never involved giving up the foods I loved. As a certified sports nutrition specialist, I build meal plans around your dietary preferences, whether vegetarian, vegan, or non-vegetarian, using real Indian foods that work for your goals.

I have a busy work schedule. Can I realistically do this?

Yes, and I understand that pressure personally. I was a practicing advocate before becoming a coach. I know what it means to have a demanding schedule that competes with your health. Programs are designed to fit the time you actually have, not an ideal version of your week.

How is this different from following a YouTube workout?

Generic content cannot account for your body, your habits, or your starting point. I provide a fully personalised plan built on your body composition data, a sports nutrition strategy calibrated to your goals, and direct accountability, the same combination that drove my own 30 kg transformation.
