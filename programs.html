What working with me actually means

Training That Is Built for Your Body

Holding an ACE CPT, a Level 4 Certified Personal Trainer qualification, and EREPS Level 4 membership, every program I write is grounded in internationally recognised standards. You do not get a generic workout sheet. You get a progressive plan designed around your current fitness level, your schedule, and your specific goal, whether that is fat loss, strength, or simply feeling better every day.

Nutrition That Works in Real Life

With a Sports Nutrition Certificate and an ACE-approved Fitness Nutrition Specialist credential, plus a full Diploma in Personal Training and Nutrition, I design meal strategies that fit around your food preferences, your culture, and your daily routine. No crash diets. No banned foods. Just a macronutrient framework you can actually sustain, built on the same principles that helped me shift from binge eating to a balanced, high-performance diet.

Movement Skills That Transfer to Life

I hold certifications in Olympic Lifting, Plyometrics, Kettlebell Training, and Suspension Training. This means the movement toolkit I draw from goes far beyond machines and treadmills. Depending on your goals and access to equipment, I can programme strength, power, mobility, and functional movement patterns that make you more capable in every area of your daily life, not just inside the gym.

Safety You Can Trust From Session One

I am CPR certified and hold a SPEFL-SC Level 4 qualification, which means your safety is built into every session before a single rep is programmed. Proper form, injury prevention, and load progression are not afterthoughts here. For beginners especially, learning to move correctly from the start is what separates short-term results from lifelong fitness habits.

A System That Adapts as You Grow

The breadth of my certifications means I am never locked into one method. As your fitness improves, your programming evolves with it. We start where you are, build a strong foundation, and progressively raise the standard as your body adapts. This is how a 120 kg sedentary lifestyle becomes 25 to 30 kg of fat lost in six months and then keeps going.

Accountability That Makes the Difference

Information alone does not create transformation. Accountability does. As your coach, I track your progress, review your form, adjust your plan when life gets in the way, and keep you focused on the long game. I went from practicing law to building this coaching practice because I saw too many people with the right intention but no consistent system. That system is what I bring to every client relationship.

Common Questions

Clarifying the coaching process for you.

I have never lifted weights before. Is this for me?

This is built for you. I started from the same place: 120 kg, no gym experience, no idea where to begin. Every program is custom-built for your current fitness level, so you learn correct form, build confidence, and progress safely without ever feeling overwhelmed.

Will I have to give up Indian food or eat bland meals?

Absolutely not. My own nutrition shifted from binge eating to a macronutrient-balanced diet, and it never involved giving up the foods I loved. As a certified sports nutrition specialist, I build meal plans around your dietary preferences, whether vegetarian, vegan, or non-vegetarian, using real Indian foods that work for your goals.

I have a busy work schedule. Can I realistically do this?

Yes, and I understand that pressure personally. I was a practicing advocate before becoming a coach. I know what it means to have a demanding schedule that competes with your health. Programs are designed to fit the time you actually have, not an ideal version of your week.

How is this different from following a YouTube workout?

Generic content cannot account for your body, your habits, or your starting point. I provide a fully personalised plan built on your body composition data, a sports nutrition strategy calibrated to your goals, and direct accountability, the same combination that drove my own 30 kg transformation.
