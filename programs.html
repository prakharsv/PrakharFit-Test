Common questions

Quick Answers.

Do I need a gym membership?

Both programs work whether you train at a gym or at home. The program is built around whatever setup you actually have access to.

Will I have to give up Indian food?

No. Your nutrition plan is built around what you actually eat - dal, sabzi, roti, paneer - restructured for your goals. Not replaced.

How much time do I need to train?

Most clients start with 3 to 4 sessions per week, 45 to 60 minutes each. The program is built around your actual available time, not an ideal schedule.

How much does it cost?

Pricing is not listed publicly because it depends on program and duration. Fill out the form and I will give you clear pricing with no obligation to commit.
