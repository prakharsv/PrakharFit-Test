Credentials

Certifications & Credentials.

Before you trust someone with your health, you deserve to know exactly who you are working with.

When I decided to become a coach, I did not settle for the minimum. The personal transformation was not enough. I needed to understand the science behind what had happened to my own body, and I needed credentials that held me to the highest standard of knowledge and safety. Every qualification below was earned for that reason.

Personal Training Credentials

The foundational qualifications that certify me to design and deliver safe, effective training programs.

PT & Nutrition Diploma

Diploma in Personal Training & Nutrition covering anatomy, programming, and dietary science.

Level 4 CPT

Certified Personal Trainer Certificate at Level 4 - the highest standard qualification in the UK framework.

ACE CPT

ACE Certified Personal Trainer - one of the most globally recognised personal training certifications in the world.

Professional Memberships

Recognised bodies that hold me to ongoing standards of practice and continued professional development.

EREPS Level 4 Member

European Register of Exercise Professionals - Level 4 membership, the European standard for qualified fitness professionals.

SPEFL-SC Level 4

Sports & Physical Education Federation of India - Level 4 certified, recognising professional standards in India's fitness sector.

Nutrition Specialisations

Training and nutrition are designed together. These qualifications are the reason I can do that effectively.

Sports Nutrition Certificate

Specialist certification covering macronutrient strategy, sports fuelling, and nutrition periodisation for athletic performance.

Fitness Nutrition Specialist

ACE-approved Fitness Nutrition Specialist covering evidence-based dietary approaches for fat loss, muscle gain, and general health.

Specialised Training Methods

Additional certifications that allow me to build varied, effective programs beyond standard resistance training.

Olympic Lifting

Certified in Olympic lifting technique and programming for explosive strength and athletic movement development.

Plyometrics

Certified in plyometric training for power development, agility, and athletic conditioning.

Kettlebell Training

Certified in kettlebell programming - one of the most effective tools for combining strength, conditioning, and mobility.

Suspension Training

Certified in suspension training methods - bodyweight-based training using instability to develop total-body strength and coordination.

Safety Certification

Non-negotiable in any coaching environment.

CPR Certified

Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation certified - because client safety is the foundation everything else is built on.
